THE PALETTES

A repertoire of pre-made palettes are available in the lab for users to explore and play with. Some palettes are based on scholarly archeological research on ancient pigments. A second category of pre-made palettes is available based on iconic paintings and art history masterpieces. A third type of available palettes is based on the top fabric patterns of the year and the most used colors and trends of the fashion world. Finally, a database of user-uploaded palettes is available for any other use to get inspiration from.

It is also possible for the user to dynamically design their own palette, including from the colors of their own skin, body, clothes, and surroundings.
