All in all, ColorColab is meant to be a highly interactive project. The user can choose the statue of their choice to begin to enjoy their experience and move around how they want. Secondly, they can directly contribute to the color palette database with their own color uploads from their own skin and clothes (and they can even input some data they would like alter users to know about the color’s origin). Additionally, users can upload their creations to the general database of “colorized” statues.
