ABOUT

ColorColab was conceived and designed by Delfina Sol Pandiani as culmination of the course "Intangible Artifacts, Cultural Heritage and Multimedia", part of the Master's degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at the Università di Bologna.



Delfina Sol Pandiani Born in Jamaica and raised in Argentina, Delfina works at the intersection of art, science and tech. She received her B.A. in Human Evolutionary Biology from Harvard University in 2017 and is currently pursuing her M.A. in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge.

.



Intangible Artifacts, Cultural Heritage and Multimedia The course, taught by Professor Sofia Pescarin, investigates intangible cultural heritage mediated by computer science and expressed under the form of practices, representations and skills that the multimedia research community recognizes as part of its identity. The main focus of the course is on embodiment, landscapes, cognition, and design of digital objects.
